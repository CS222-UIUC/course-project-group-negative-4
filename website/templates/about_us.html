About Us

Have you ever dreaded selecting your courses each semester? ICD was built by UIUC students to help students reduce this dread. For a given course, ICD provides you with all the necessary data such as recent semester grade distributions and both links to RateMyProfessor ratings and Reddit posts. In summary, this platform is the all in one place to help students make informed decisions regarding course selection.
